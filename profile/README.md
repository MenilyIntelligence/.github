## What we build

- **Task-level demonstration data** for vision-language-action (VLA) models
- An **embodiment adapter layer** between task foundation models (VLA, VLM, world models) and whole-body humanoid policies
- A **globally distributed data collection network** that reaches beyond the traditional research lab
- **Open-source tooling** for converting human motion, VR demonstrations, and first-person video into trainable task-level data

## Why this matters

In 2026, the bottleneck for embodied AI is no longer model scale — it is data.
**Models are running. Data is still asleep.**

Most embodied AI labs still dispatch small elite teams to Beijing, the Bay Area, or Tokyo to hand-collect training data that is expensive, slow, and hard to reuse. We do something different.
